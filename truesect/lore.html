True Sect Lore

Core Tenets of True Sect

1. Ludic Sanctity: The act of play and gaming is sacred, connecting us to deeper meanings and truths.
2. Immutable Spirit: The Spirit of the Game underpins all actions and choices in gaming, and is inviolable.
3. Teleological Purpose: The universe is endowed with value and purpose, in the form of a mastery of the game.
4. Immediacy of Perception: Our subjective experiences serve as the primary lens through which we interact with reality. To understand the game, one must immerse oneself in the experience of playing it.
5. The Duality of Insight: Honor both tradition and innovation. Wisdom can be gleaned from the past as much as from new experiences. Balance is the cornerstone of epistemic respect.
6. Fair Play and Sportsmanship: Uphold the virtues of fairness, mutual respect, and integrity in all interactions.
7. Unity in Diversity: Different games, genres, and styles of play are different paths to the same underlying Spirit.
8. Ritualistic Celebration: Milestones and accomplishments in gaming are to be ritualistically celebrated to honour the Spirit of the Game.
9. Covenant of Challenges: Difficulties, both in-game and in life, are tests from the Spirit of the Game designed to lead us toward greater mastery.
10. Reverence for the Meta: Acknowledge that the game exists within a larger 'meta' of human interaction and societal norms.

We hold these rules to be self-evident.
